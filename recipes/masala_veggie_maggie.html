Masala Veggie Maggie

Today we are going to see about the recipe of the our beloved food, Maggie !!!

Description

Now of course we have all seen and eaten the typical, out of the box maggie straight from nestle being cooked UNDER 2 MINUTES !!, but of course when we have some nice time in our hand we would like to add a bit of a twist on it, for that twist of course we are gonna need more than just a pack of frozen noodles and a packet of Masala!

Ingredients

  • Standard 2 packs of nestle maggie
  • Standatd 2 packs of Maggie masala(the one included in the pack itself)
  • Butter/Oil(preferbly olive oil)
  • Chopped carrots
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Green peas
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Put the oil/butter in the heating pan
  2. When the oil/butter is properly heated, add our chopped onion in it and keep stirring until golden
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and wait for them to become pulpy
  4. When the tomatoes are pulpy and smooth, add carrots and peas in it
  5. Add Masala in the mixture, and stirr well
  6. When veggies and masala is properly mixed add water in it and close the lid, wait for boil to come
  7. When the pan starts to boil, at last add the frozen noodles in it and agian close the lid
  8. Wait for noodles to soak the water and close gas, add cheese in on the maggie, and serve hot !
